Real estate analytics primer

Median sale price is robust to outliers, while average price can be skewed by luxury transactions.
Price per square foot can be useful for quick comparisons across nearby properties.
Days-on-market helps estimate listing liquidity and buyer demand.

When building predictive models:
- Keep train and test periods temporally consistent.
- Add location proxies such as neighborhood and school quality indices.
- Evaluate with MAE and RMSE for regression tasks.
- Document feature drift if market conditions change.
