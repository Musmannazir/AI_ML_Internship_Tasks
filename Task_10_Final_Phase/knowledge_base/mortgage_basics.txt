Mortgage basics

A mortgage payment usually includes principal, interest, taxes, and insurance (PITI).
Higher interest rates increase monthly payments and reduce maximum affordable loan size.
Loan-to-value ratio (LTV) compares the loan amount to property value.
Lower LTV generally reduces lender risk and may improve approval chances.

Credit score, debt-to-income ratio, and employment stability are major underwriting factors.
Borrowers should compare fixed-rate and adjustable-rate products before selecting a loan.
